## Sample Query

```sql
SELECT
    product,
    SUM(total_price) AS revenue
FROM sales_data
GROUP BY product
ORDER BY revenue DESC;
```

# Business Insights Summary

Through both Excel and SQL analysis, the following insights were discovered:

### Sales Performance

* High-value products generated the majority of revenue.
* Electronics and furniture categories showed strong sales performance.

### Customer Behavior

* Customers purchased an average of 3 items per order.
* Digital payment methods were preferred over traditional methods.

### Operational Performance

* High cancellation and return rates suggest opportunities for process improvement.
* Customer satisfaction and order fulfillment should be further optimized.

### Revenue Trends

* Revenue distribution was positively skewed by several high-value transactions.
* Premium products contributed significantly to overall business performance.
